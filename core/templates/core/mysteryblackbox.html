Mystery Black Box Investigation

by:Khady Gaye

BACKGROUND:

The Mystery Black Box is a big black rectangle case with a funnel tube coming out from the top of it and a cylinder tube coming out from the bottom. The goal in this investigation is to figure out how to make a similar model and try to replicate the case without knowing what is inside of the mystery black box.

CLAIM:

The mystery black box could contain a system with tipping cups and piping/tubes to guide the liquid poured into the funnel to the cylinder tube at the end.

EVIDENCE:

The mystery black box is a closed case system that is modeled to show a fluid moving from pouring a liquid into a funnel and coming out from a cylinder tube. The main initial observation was an unknown tubing system. My approach to figuring out how it works was observing and analyzing the funnel and cylinder tube which are the models key structures. I tested the model by putting 800mL of water into the funnel and got back 450mL after the water was poured out of the cylinder tube in 7 secs.
